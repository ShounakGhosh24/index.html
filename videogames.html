Overwatch


Overwatch is currently my favorite game. I got it over a year ago and I play almost everyday. The game is a fps ( First Person Shooter ). Even though there are many gamemodes with different playstyles, most games are a 6v6. There are currently 32 playable heroes in Overwatch, with very different backstories and abilities. Each of them fall into one of three categories - Tank, Damage, or Support. With many maps from around the world several gamemodes, this game is amazing and I'm hoping to get better at it. (Overwatch 2 is also coming out soon)
